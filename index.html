Taj Mahal
Also called Red Fort
Location Agra, Uttar Pradesh
Area 94 acres (38 ha)
Built 1565-1573
Built for Akbar
Architectural style(s) Mughal
UNESCO World Heritage site. 1983

  • Taj mahal is one of Seven Wonders of the World
  • It is a white Marble Mughal Architecture, and is called the “Jewel of Muslim Art in India”.
  • The Taj Mahal was build by emperor Shah Jahan in the memory of his favourite wife Mumtaj Mahal.
  • It is located at the bank of river Yamuna in Agra.
  • It was completed in 1653 with then estimated cost of 32 million Indian rupees which would today stand upto 58 billion Indian rupees.
  • This is probably the monument that is most recognised the world over for its association with the heritage of India.
